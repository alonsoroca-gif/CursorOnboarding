> **Purpose:** This doc records the **company PM Performance Expectations and PM Onboarding** that form the heart of the Cursor-for-PMs onboarding app. The onboarding is designed to directly or indirectly support these expectations. ## PM Performance Expectations (summary)

- **ACV updates & at-risk ACV:** Weekly Thursday noon via DOMO and Slack; proactive risk ID and structured reporting.
- **Moving ACV:** Daily as needed; approvals for moves above $250K; NITI criteria and escalation.
- **Billing:** Daily as needed; requests 4 business days before month-end; delay process and approvals.
- **Unscheduled ACV:** Weekly review so all ACV is scheduled before implementation.
- **Monthly ACV forecast & reconciliation:** Forecast by 5th; reconciliation and accuracy targets.
- **Project health & Hive:** Weekly client touchpoints, project accuracy, zero stuck/overdue tasks, daily time tracking per SOP.
- **Self review:** Quarterly self-evaluation with rubric; discussion in 1:1s.
- **Professional skillset & client expectations:** Meeting professionalism, proactive communication, **Slack within 4 hours / email within 24 hours**, keep managers informed of risks and blockers.
- **Role expectations:** Follow processes; delegate to Sales, Consulting, Support/CSM, Specialists; align with department goals and SOPs.

---

## PM Onboarding (day-to-day)

- **Day in the life:** Progress review, stand-ups, asset writing, email/ask channel, bug triage, backlog grooming, discovery, strategic planning, Loom wrap-up.
- **Discovery & sales:** Field- and product-sponsored forms; prep, execution, documentation.
- **Jira:** Project management, issue tracking, **weekly release trackers**, **communicating delays**.
- **Partners:** Track engagements, involve key stakeholders, regular check-ins.

---

## PM Backup and Transition SOP

- Identify need for backup/transition; backup doc with stakeholders; transition meetings (Hive, Client Admin, Slack, calendars); return meetings to review status.

---

## Sync & integrations (e.g. PM Sync)

- Monitor sync health, understand errors, corrective actions and escalation when PMs oversee integrations.
